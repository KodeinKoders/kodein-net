Salomon Brys

Founder

Salomon has been coding for more than 20 years (he started at age 12), and working on mobile applications as soon as smartphone existed. Deeply rooted in the open source philosophy, he loves making tools and libraries, always contributing to the industry's state of the art. He's always looking for the future of programming, and has been contributing to Kotlin & Kotlin/Native since their inception.


He dances 6 beats rock, flies small planes, and loooves boardgames.

Nerd on Twitter, Linkedin, Github

Romain Boisselle

Founder

Romain has been coding for almost 15 years. For some times, he has been working on server side architectures with Java, then with Kotlin since its early days. He added a new string to its bow at KotlinConf 2018, where he met Salomon and started contributing to Kodein-DI, the very first Kotlin/Multiplatform community library.


He loves wild spaces, mountain hikes and climbing.
