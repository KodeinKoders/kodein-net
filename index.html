Who are the Kodein Koders?

We are tech lovers & experts driven by our passion for Kotlin/Multiplatform.
With this technology, we develop complex applications and share meaningful code between platforms, whether the backend server (based on Spring or Ktor) or the multiple frontends (Android, iOS, Desktop, Web).

We create multiplatform mobile application architectures, modernize existing systems, or help teams reach their development goals with Kotlin, everywhere you need.

Kodein Framework: Open Source at our core

The KODEINFramework is the first Open-Source multiplatform mobile framework written specifically for Kotlin & Kotlin/Multiplatform. It allows mobile application creators to focus on their application business and quality.

We firmly believe that open-sourcing non business-critical components and contributing to existing open-source projects leads to better software.
Kodein Koders is heavily invested in using and contributing to Open Source initiatives. The Kotlin language & runtime as well as the Kodein Framework: everything is open!

Training: Jetbrains Certified

The Kotlin language, coroutines asynchronicity, the Kotlin/Multiplatform infrastructure, Kotlin/Native specifics: trying to understand them all can be daunting.

KODEINKoders is the first European company to be certified by JetBrains for Kotlin training. It is now the first training provider in Europe specialised on Kotlin/Multiplatform technologies.

We always tailor our training workshops for the team we are addressing, and use precise teaching material to make teams efficient in a matter of days.
